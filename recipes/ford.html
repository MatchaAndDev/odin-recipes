Ford Cocktail

Description

Styled after a classic gin Martini, this variation invites a bit of Bénédictine into the mix.

Ingredients

  • 2oz Gray Whale Gin
  • 1oz dry vermouth
  • 1/3oz Bénédictine D.O.M.
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • orange peel

Tools

  • mixing glass
  • barspoon
  • stainer

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients mixing glass
  2. Add ice to mixing glass
  3. Use barspoon to stir
  4. Strain into chilled coupe
  5. Garnish by expressing the orange oils over the drink then add to cocktail
