Gin Martini

Description

This gin martini recipe is simple, sophisticated and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2oz gin
  • 1/2oz dry vermouth
  • 1 dashes orange bitters
  • lemon twist or 1 or 3 olives, for garnish

Tools

  • mixing glass
  • barspoon
  • stainer

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients into mixing glass
  2. Stir vigorously for 15 seconds
  3. Strain into chilled coupe or Martini glass
  4. Garnish with lemon twist or olives
